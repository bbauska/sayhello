About

I'm Brian Bauska. Graduated 1986 from Central Washington University. My passion besides family is databases. DDL, DML, DCL. Wrote my first database in 1983 using Pascal on a DEC/VAX VMS 785. Since, I've worked with HP3000 Image databases, IBM AS400/Oracle databases, DEC RDBMS, SQL, and warehouse management system and too many to list. All relational.
